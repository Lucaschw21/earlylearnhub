EarlyLearn Hub: Bridging Gaps in Pre-Primary Education

Company Background

The EarlyLearn Hub project is done by 4 UTeM (Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka) students which are taking the Web Application Development course. This project emerges as a beacon of hope and innovation to address a pervasive issue that transcends geographical boundaries and economic disparities: the unequal access to quality education for children as they embark on their educational journey in kindergarten. In many corners of the world, not all children are privileged with the same educational resources, setting the stage for disparities that can have far-reaching consequences.
